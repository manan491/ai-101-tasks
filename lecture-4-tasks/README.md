# Pressure Point 🌡️

**Live app:** https://pressure-point.netlify.app

A simple web app that checks the air pressure for the next 2 days and warns you if a big change is coming.

## Why this app?

Some people get **headaches (migraines)** or **joint pain** when the weather changes. The real reason is often the **air pressure** going up or down fast. Most people don't know this — and they don't know it can be checked before it happens.

Pressure Point shows you the air pressure for the next **48 hours** and tells you in simple words if a bad time is coming. So you can be ready — take medicine early, drink water, or take it easy that day.

## How to use it

1. Open the app: https://pressure-point.netlify.app
2. Type your city name (like *Lahore* or *Karachi*) and press **Check**.
3. The app shows you a chart and a simple message — like *"Sharp pressure drop tomorrow afternoon"* or *"Stable, low risk."*

You can also tap **Use my location** instead of typing a city.

## What it shows

- A 48-hour air pressure chart
- A plain warning if a big swing is coming
- The pressure right now, and the biggest change ahead

## Built with

- One single HTML file — no setup, no installation
- Plain JavaScript and SVG (no extra libraries)
- Free [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com) weather data — no API key needed
