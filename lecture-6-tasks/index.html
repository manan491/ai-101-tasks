Abdul Manan Khalid

I build AI-native systems where LLMs and agents are the software — not just the assistant. Training through Panaversity's Agent Factory, I design, deploy, and monetize Digital FTEs: reliable AI workers that run around the clock, built with spec-driven workflows and verified before they ship. My foundation is Mechatronics Engineering — control systems and hardware-software integration — which I bring to agent design as a bias toward systems that are observable, testable, and actually work in production. I work across the bilingual AI-native stack, Python for reasoning and TypeScript for interaction, and I care as much about how an agent fails and recovers as about what it does when everything goes right.

01 About

Specs in, working systems out.

I now apply that to agentic AI: designing autonomous agents that reason, act, and collaborate. I like turning a clear specification into a deployed system, and I treat verification as part of the build, not an afterthought.
